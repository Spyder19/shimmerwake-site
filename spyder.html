Spyder (leaning back, grinning): “Yeah, about that—let’s get one thing straight.” The air shifts as Spyder stands, slowly pacing around the table.

Spyder: “The only reason I take your ‘suggestions’ is because I agreed to take your money. That’s it. You don’t own me.”

Spyder yells: "Free pie from the Universe!"

Plates were grabbed. Forks raised in a temporary, sticky truce. Wayne, shaking his head as the lock cleared: “Are you ever going to explain how you do that?”

Spyder, grinning, replied, “It’s not magic—it’s just a knack for noticing what everyone’s really hungry for.”

Spyder’s ad read: "Seeking: Crew for adventure. Must be unflappable, inventive, tolerant of bad coffee, odd music, and uncertain plumbing. No boating experience required—lack of it preferred, really. This will not be on the test. Must provide your own hat."
