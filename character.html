Characters of the Dauntless Expedition

Spyder

Captain of the Dauntless, the adventurous leader seeking to unravel the mysteries of portals and legends.

"Whatever these portals are, they’re bigger than lakes, bigger than monsters."

Vanessa

A fearless navigator, bridging the gap between myths and reality with her intuitive understanding of the unknown.

"A portal needs guardians as much as it needs doors."

Wayne

Engineer and strategist, Wayne navigates the balance between technology and the supernatural with wit and skepticism.

"If you can reboot an army surplus laptop, you're in. Welcome aboard, matey."

Champ

The enigmatic lake guardian, straddling the worlds of legend and reality as a symbol of ancient secrets.

"All this time—the monster, the myth, the madness. Not two creatures. One."

FRED

The multipurpose robotic assistant, always ready with engineering insights and a dash of humor.

"Would you like me to categorize this incident as an ‘unexpected success’ or an ‘avoidable mishap’?"

Dauntless

The steadfast vessel that carries the crew through portals and across legendary paths.

"On Dauntless, the question is less 'where' than 'when do we stop gawking and start telling the folks back home?'"

Gazoo

The yet-to-be-completed drone companion, destined to be an amusingly annoying and insightful element of the crew.

"When the story seems impossible, check the stars twice."
